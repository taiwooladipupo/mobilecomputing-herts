4. CRITIQUE

There are several e-commerce and a number of gift apps available for download either free or paid on both iOS and Android app stores. From my research, I discovered there are more e-commerce apps than gift apps but I did find a very good gift app that is somewhat similar to the idea of PassDown, the app name is Giftagram and its url is https://apps.apple.com/us/app/giftagram-gifting-made-easy/id647383882.

KEY FEATURES

  1. Contact integration: get birthday reminders and add personalized messaging
  2. Freshly curated gifts: new gifts added weekly and handpicked by experts
  3. Variety of categories: shop gifts for women or men from categories like tech/gadgets, sweets/desserts, alcohol, flowers/plants, apothecary, gourmet, children and more
