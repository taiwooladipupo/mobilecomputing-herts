PASSDOWN

1. Goal of the final app

​PassDown is the name of the final app to be developed, its goal is to bridge the gap between privileged and less-privileged families in any given community in terms of sharing basic needs like food, clothes, shoes, books and other school supplies.
 
Basically, there are hundreds of families who have food items, clothes, shoes and school items that their children don't need any more and would not mind passing down these items to those who need them but the gap exist between these two such that what the less privileged families critically need don’t get to them.
 
So, the app should connect both end-users together, the first uploads the items they want to give out, while the latter browses the items and simply go shopping. The receiving family then choose how they want the items delivered at checkout. Geo-fencing and geo-location will be implemented such that end-users can only operate within their state or county.
 
Other features will be for the end-users to be able to create a wish-list and have other users fulfil one or all of the list, also ability to be able to track your “order”.
FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
  • User Account Registration and User Login
  • Create a wish-list
  • Upload gift items 
  • Browse gift items
  • Add gift items to shopping cart and checkout
  • Track order
  • Push Notifications
  • Email Notifications
  • Geo-Location/ Geo-Fencing

NON- FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
  • Interactive and extremely user-friendly design and navigation
  • Responsiveness
